The Full Story

John W. (Bill) Fowler founded Anchor Strategies, Inc. in 2011 to provide
consulting services and expert testimony for a large, western Canadian utility.
Over the past 40 years, Bill has worked with Fortune 500 companies on some of
the largest and complex outsourcing agreements. In an industry full of hype and
overpromises, Anchor Strategies focuses on the fundamentals. A rigorous
process, sound financial modeling, and building collaboration of partners with
divergent goals.
